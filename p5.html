Audi x Yohji Yamamoto

An historical event in Berlin, on the night of the 25th of April 2013, something magical and unique happened under the ceiling of the St Agnes church and before the eyes of 250 lucky people. Yohji Yamamoto celebrated 40 years of making clothes by creating a truly unrepeatable spectacle: a catwalk of the most beloved and iconic pieces of Yamamoto's entire career. the intensity of the models, the dramatic beauty of the music, the theatre, the act. it's not easy to find appropriate words to describe such a sublime moment. Much like ghosts, the clothes appeared to come to life in a resurrection of time, cutting the air and engraving an everlasting memory of their passage through the minds of the audience.
